Description

The annual Volunteer Fair gives University of Manitoba students the chance to connect directly with dozens of local non-profit and charitable organizations who are actively recruiting volunteers for a wide range of community initiatives. This event helps students discover meaningful volunteer opportunities that can build skills, expand networks, and contribute to their Experience Record.
